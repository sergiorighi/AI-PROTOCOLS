# Security Policy

## Status

AI-PROTOCOLS is an experimental documentation repository. It does not distribute a runtime, bridge, server, client, or executable binary.

## Reporting Issues

If you find a security issue related to repository content, open an issue with:

- affected file.
- objective risk description.
- expected impact.
- suggested correction, if any.

Do not publish secrets, tokens, personal data, or third-party material in issues.

## Scope

In scope:

- documents that could lead to unsafe use.
- examples that expose sensitive data.
- instructions that blur runtime, transport, or authentication limits.

Out of scope:

- vulnerabilities in external tools.
- FlowLine implementations or other products outside this repository.
- behavior of AI models in third-party services.

## Support

No security SLA is defined during this experimental phase. Reports will be handled according to maintainer availability.
