# Code of Conduct

## Commitment

Participants should maintain respectful, objective collaboration focused on technical work.

## Expected Behavior

- Disagree with focus on facts, scope, and verifiable criteria.
- Assume good faith without ignoring technical risks.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, provocation, or discriminatory language.
- Respect the project's scope limits.
- Correct information when it is demonstrably inaccurate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or intimidation.
- Discriminatory or sexualized comments.
- Exposure of private information without permission.
- Pressure to accept changes outside the stated scope.
- Conduct that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, and discussions to preserve this code of conduct. Measures may include warnings, editing or closing discussions, temporary blocking, or banning.

## Contact

Use the repository issue channels to report conduct problems when it is safe to do so. For sensitive cases, use the private channel indicated by the project maintainer when available.
